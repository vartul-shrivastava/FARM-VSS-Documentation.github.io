FAQ

Q1: What file formats are supported for upload?

A1: FARM-VSS supports .csv, .xls, and .xlsx file formats.

Q2: How do I set the AI model for summary generation?

A2: Navigate to the AI Model section in the header, select your desired model from the dropdown, and click “Set Model.”

Q3: What should I do if Ollama is not detected?

A3: Ensure that Ollama is installed and running on your system. Check your system’s PATH to verify that Ollama is accessible.

Q4: Can I customize the AI prompt used for summary generation?

A4: Yes, click on “Modify Prompt” to customize the instructions sent to the AI model. Ensure that the {rules} placeholder is included in your prompt.

Q5: How do I reset the modified AI prompt to its default state?

A5: Click on “Reset Prompt” in the Modify Prompt modal to revert to the default prompt.
